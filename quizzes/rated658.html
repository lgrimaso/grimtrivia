What year did the popular sitcom The Cosby Show leave the airwaves?

My wife was a ding-bat and my son-in-law was a meat-head. Me? I just worked on a loading dock, and drove a cab part time.

From 1966-1969 I captained The Starship Enterprise on what was supposed to be a 5-year mission.

I was a New York City sewer worker and Ralph and Alice Kramdens upstairs neighbor on The Honeymooners.

From 1964 to 1972, I played the prettiest witch on TV. At least my husband Darrin thought so.

From 1970-1975 my sloppy sportswriter roommate and I lived in a luxury apartment in New York City on the TV comedy The Odd Couple.

I played the good-hearted, but dim-witted 1st mate on Gilligans Island.

From 1984-1992 I played, Heathcliff Huxtable, and was one of Americas favorite TV dads.

My voice is the same as Dan Castellanetas, but so are Barney Gumbles, Krusty the Clowns, and Mayor Quimbys on the same TV show.
