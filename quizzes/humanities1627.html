humanities

Athena was the Greek equivalent of the goddess Minerva.

Nike was a goddess, who personified triumph and victory (Greek mythology). Ironically, she was paralyzed and could not move her legs.

Helicon is the name of an ancient lake, where the Muses were born.

Calliope was the muse for music.

Marduk was a Mesopotamian deity.

Svarog is the highest god of the pantheon (Slavic mythology) and the god of thunder and lightning.

The creature from Slavic mythology known as Zmey actually resembles a dragon.

Loki is the god of mischief of Norse mythology.

One of these words has mid-17th century Latin origins and is a synonym of the word, wavy.
