history

In the age of the Vikings, Viking ships were the most technologically advanced.

In Viking culture all men were expected to carry weapons.

Loki was the Viking God of lies.

The first recorded Viking raid in Britain was in 763.

Leif Eriksson also known as Leif the Lucky is thought to have been the first European to land in North America.

The father of all Norse or Viking gods was Thor the God of Thunder.

WWII began on September 1, 1939, when Germany invaded this country.

The first underground metro in North America, the Boston subway, was opened on September 1 of this year.

On September 1, 1983 Korean Air Flight KAL-007 was shot down after entering Soviet airspace (this is true). Some of the passengers and crew survived the crash.
